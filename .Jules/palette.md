## 2024-04-23 - Focus states and ARIA labels on framer-motion Material Icons buttons
**Learning:** `motion.button` components wrapping `material-symbols-outlined` spans require specific accessibility handling. The `aria-hidden="true"` attribute must be applied to the inner `span` to prevent screen readers from announcing ligatures (like "forum" or "send"). Furthermore, `motion.button` naturally accepts Tailwind's `focus-visible` classes (e.g., `focus-visible:ring-2`), providing robust keyboard accessibility without complex Framer Motion state management.
**Action:** When implementing new interactive elements using Framer Motion and Material Icons, always ensure `aria-label` is on the button, `aria-hidden="true"` is on the icon span, and `focus-visible` utility classes are included for keyboard support.
